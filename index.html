24 Hour Care

Independently living at home is something we all want to do for as long as possible but we all need help with different tasks now and then.

What is 24 hour care?

24 hour care is a personalised service that allows you to receive taiored care services in your own home.

You have the opportunity to maintain your regular lifestyle and daily activities with the help of a trained/certified carer.

Our service is flexible and we match the care and the care worker to you. Every visit starts with an assessment of what you need.

We have a variety of packages available for you to chose from.

Costs will then be discussed once assessments have been done as we would need to find the best price for your care package.

Mental Health-care

We seek to help individuals with a learning disability live as full a life as possible.

What is mental health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices.

Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.

Most types of mental distress or mental illness fall into one of two main types: psychosis or neurosis. Some people may have dual diagnosis (two conditions).

Supported Living


We can help if can help if you don't want to live in residential care but you're finding it difficult to cope at home.


What is supported living?

Supported living is used to describe the arrangement whereby someone who already has ,or who wants their own tenancy or own home, also has support from a “Care and Support” provider to help them live as independently and safely as possible.

We can help if you don't want to live in residential care but you're finding it difficult to cope at home.

Our care service can be provided for as long as you need us. This varies from short respite ‘sitting’ for a few hours at a time, through to several visits a day on a long term basis to deliver personal care.

The cost of this package will depend on the level of care that you require us to provide and also varies geographically.
